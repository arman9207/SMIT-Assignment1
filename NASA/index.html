A Young, Eruptive Star

A team of astronomers has wielded the Hubble Space Telescope's
ultraviolet capabilities to learn more about the interaction between the
young star FU Orionis' stellar surface and the accretion disk that has been
dumping gas onto the growing star for nearly 90 years.
